Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how GoE Technologies ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our website or services.

Where applicable (for users in the EU/EEA/UK), our legal bases for processing include consent, performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests (such as improving and securing the service). For contractual processing (e.g., payments), we process data necessary to perform the contract.

We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests from public authorities, enforce our terms, or protect rights. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We implement appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses where required and rely on adequacy decisions when available.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies. Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use cookies for the following categories:

  • Essential: necessary for the website to function (e.g., session cookies).
  • Analytics: used to understand how visitors use the site (e.g., Google Analytics). Retained for 24 months by default.
  • Marketing: used to deliver relevant advertising and measure campaigns.

Opt-out: You can control cookies through your browser settings or opt out of analytics cookies via available tools such as Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy and to comply with legal obligations. Typical retention periods:

  • Transactional and support data: 3 years
  • Analytics and logs: 24 months
  • Backups: up to 90 days

Users' Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

  • Access: request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: request limitation of processing
  • Portability: receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: object to certain processing (e.g., direct marketing)

To exercise these rights, contact us at robertaryehceo@goetechnologies.org. We will verify your identity and aim to respond within 30 days. If additional time is required, we will inform you.

Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will assess the risk to individuals and notify affected users and supervisory authorities when legally required. Where required by law, we will notify within 72 hours of becoming aware of a notifiable breach.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information.
